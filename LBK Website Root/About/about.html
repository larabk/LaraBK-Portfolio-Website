A little blurb about me...

I made new keys - because old keys do not open new doors.

For as long as I can remember, my life revolved around my passion for technology and building. As a kid, I'd take apart computers and audio electronics just to see how they worked and "Frankenstein" them back together. This curiosity lead me to an 18 year career in the AudioVisual industry. I started as an audio engineer working live events, progressed through various roles to a Project Manager for an AV organization, and simultaneously owned and operated a live event production/installation company.

As a life-long learner, I was searching for better ways to apply my favorite skills within different technologies. Through this exploration and as my interests grew, I discovered my absolute love of programming. Software Development encompasses all that I am passionate about; technology, creativity, problem solving, and building.

I'm currently enrolled at Tech Elevator and after graduation I'll be looking for a Junior Software Development role. My goal is to make meaningful contributions in the workplace by expanding on the technical skills I've gained while bringing with me the diverse skillset I've acquired along the way.
